The IndiaAI Mission

Empowering the nation through Artificial Intelligence and Digital Innovation

📖 About

Mission Overview

The IndiaAI Mission is a comprehensive initiative aimed at catalyzing the artificial intelligence ecosystem in India. Designed to democratize AI technology, the mission focuses on capacity building, infrastructure development, and fostering research.

Our Vision

To establish India as a global hub for AI innovation and to utilize AI for driving inclusive growth, enhancing governance, and solving large-scale societal challenges across sectors such as healthcare, agriculture, and education.

Role of NIELIT

As a key implementing agency, the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT) is executing a nationwide capacity-building program. We are establishing IndiaAI & Data Labs across 27 centers in 22 states to train 10,800 candidates over 3 years.
